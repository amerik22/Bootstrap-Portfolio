About Me

I am orginally from the Chicago area and moved to Denver in 2015. I have been working in marketing for over 10 years. In more recent postions I have gotten the chance to learn basic HTML for editing webpages and emails. I found that the most enjoyable part of my day was when I was coding. So I have decided to deepen my knowledge and change my career path towards web development.

What draws me to web development is the opprtunity to have a career where I can be a forever learner. Getting the chance to learn everyday makes for a fulfuling career. I also enjoy that a large part of the job is problem solving. There is nothing more exciting when you see your code come to life!

In my spare time I love to hike, check out live music, binge shows on netflix, and cook, eat and drink all the yummy things.
